About the Conference
The Department of Mathematics, Bharathidasan University is organizing an International Conference in Discrete Mathematics (ICDM- 2025) during December 11 - 13, 2025. The main aim of this conference is to nurture learning and research in diverse aspects of Discrete Mathematics, including Graph Theory and Coding Theory. With this in view, the conference proposes to bring leading experts from different countries and young researchers from different parts of our country to a common platform thereby providing a scope for exchanging their views and exploring recent developments in the theme of the conference. This conference aims to give special importance to young researchers - Ph. D. Scholars, Post-doctoral fellows in Discrete Mathematics in India, emphasizing creativity and nurturing emerging talent.
Target Audience
The Conference welcome research scholars, faculty members and Industry professionals who are interested in exploring the latest advancements in Discrete Mathematics along with their cutting-edge applications.
About the Department
The Department of Mathemtics started functioning in 1963. The Department of Mathematics presently offers M. Sc., and Ph. D. Programmes in Mathematics. Over the years, the Department has been making a steady growth in teaching and research and also conducting regularly various academic activities. Many outstanding mathematicians from India and abroad have visited the department and delivered lectures. The Department takes an integrated and innovative approach in teaching and training the students. As a result 2 to 4 students used to clear CSIR-JRF/NET Exam every year. Apart from teaching, the faculty members are carrying out research in the frontier areas of Mathematics including
  • Discrete Mathematics(including Coding Theory)
  • Functional Analysis
  • Differential Equations
  • Numerical Analysis
  • Real Analysis
  • Difference Equations
  • Dynamical Systems.
Recently, researchers in the Department have also shown interest in Cryptology. The staff of the department have been involving themselves in the Outreach program that is being organized jointly by the Ramanujan Mathematical Society and National College, Tiruchirappalli over the past seven years. The aim of this program is to teach the foundations of mathematics. As a by-product of this program, many participants had cleared the NET and SET exams.

Reaching BDU
Bharathidasan University Main Campus is located on the Tiruchirappalli - Pudukkottai highway (NH210), about 18km from Tiruchirappalli Railway Junction / Central Bus Stand and 10km from Tiruchirappalli Airport.
  • By Bus: You can reach the campus by taking a bus going towards Pudukkottai or Keeranur from the bus stand /railway station/ airport. The fare is approximately INR 20. State run buses (colored in pink) are free of charge for women passengers.
  • By Taxi: You can also hire a taxi from the bus stand/ railway station/airport to reach the campus. The approximate fare is INR 600
